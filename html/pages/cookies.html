What are cookies and what do they do?

Cookies are small files that are created and saved on your device when you visit a website or application. They are used to make your online experience easier by recording your browsing information on the website you visit. By using cookies, websites can keep you logged in, remember your usage preferences or offer you appropriate content.

Cookies are divided into “session cookies” and “permanent cookies” according to their duration of use. Session cookies expire when you close your browser. Permanent cookies will continue to be stored on your hard drive. You can delete permanent cookie files at any time from the temporary internet files directory on your device or through your browser history.

Our site may contain cookies belonging to YBK, as well as cookies belonging to third parties that we cooperate with. For example, we may link to our official accounts on Facebook or Twitter for our visitors, or to a video we have shared on YouTube.
