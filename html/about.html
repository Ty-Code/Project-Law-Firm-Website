About Us

Yağcı-Bulut-Konukpay (YBK) Attorney Partnership is an independent law firm based in Istanbul and Izmir. YBK Attorney Partnership provides legal services to reputable companies and individuals. All our partners and counsels are highly respected and experienced in their fields of practice.

YBK Attorney Partnership is dedicated to provide clients innovative and effective legal support in the quickest way. The firm’s approach is to provide suitable and creative solutions to its clients by putting their needs and disputes first and analyzing them correctly. It is aimed to reach the objective by evaluating all possible scenarios and risks with an attentive teamwork in all cases and requests submitted by the courts, public authorities and organizations. At the same time, great importance is attached to the legal consultancy services in order to make clients best on their own fields. With a view to identify possible risks in advance, the team follows up legal and sectoral changes and developments at national and international level with meticulous care and daily informs clients.

Our practice boasts a wide range of sectors including Retail and Consumer, E-commerce, Technology and Telecommunications, Insurance, Healthcare, Fintech, Real Estate, Financial Services, Automotive. YBK Attorney Partnership provides legal services in Turkish, English, French, and Russian languages.
