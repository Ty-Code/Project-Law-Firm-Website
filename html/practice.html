Competition

Competition law affect more areas of business than companies may think. YBK Attorney Partnership is well-positioned to help clients respond to its concerns and obligations. The practice takes a multi-dimensional approach, combining legal expertise in competition law with deep industry sector knowledge. This enables to support clients on all aspects of competition law issues.

YBK Attorney Partnership mainly advises clients on merger and acquisition notifications, investigation of abuse of dominant positions and cartels, vertical and horizontal cooperation agreements, review of commercial agreements, antitrust compliance and audit.

YBK Attorney Partnership is also keen to follow main developments and draft legislations relevant to EU competition law. This avoids any risks on large scale multi-jurisdictional projects.

Intellectual Property

The importance of intellectual property law is growing every day in today’s rapid development of international trade in the globalized world where the requirement for companies to protect the distinguishing traits of their own products and services is increasing.

YBK Attorney Partnership provide consultancy services to clients in all areas of this field, especially trademark, copyright and, industrial design. Our IP services cover license agreements, protection of trade secrets, and unfair competition. In addition, we follow up the cases arising from various disputes. and represent our clients before the Turkish Patent and Trademark Office.

Our services include patent validation procedures, trademark registration applications, design registration applications, IP portfolio management, plant breeder rights, customs protection, negotiating and drafting copyright agreements including licenses, assignments, utilization, maintenance and improvement and outsourcing agreements.

Administrative and Tax

YBK Attorney Partnership serves clients on relations with administrative authorities, administrative filings and applications for objection, cancellation of an administrative act, disputes relating to the Municipality Law, compensation claims filed due to loss or injury suffered as a consequence of an administrative action (full remedy actions). We represent our clients before the administrative courts of first instance and the State Council in both cancellation and damages actions.

We also provide consultancy to our domestic and foreign clients on concession agreements, build-operate-transfer agreements, privatizations, Public Private Partnership (PPP).

In addition, our team of experts provides a high-quality and unique service to our clients in the field of Tax Law. This area includes services such as litigation before Tax Courts, cancellation of any tax and duty accruals, tax examinations procedures, conciliation of tax penalties, tax structuring, tax dispute resolution and risk determination, reconciliations and other tax law related matters.

Our tax law services cover double taxation agreements (DTA), corporate taxes, transfer pricing, tax planning, customs legislation including customs smuggling penalties and duties, and risk assessments in order to mitigate possible risks.

Labor and Social Security

YBK Attorney Partnership provides consultancy on the non-compete agreements (NCA) and non-solicitation agreements (NSA), penalty clauses in employment agreements and, protection of employee’s personal data.

We provide a full range of legal services on the process of the termination of the employment agreement (termination, resignation, mutual termination, termination for the causes of retirement, marriage or military services etc.), organization of the holiday entitlements (annual leave etc.), relations between primary employer and subcontractor, social security and occupational safety, assignment of employment agreements, labour litigations, collective bargainings, payment of bonuses and premiums, providing social security consultancy in scope of social rights, preparation of reports and legal advisory in scope of  general health insurance under the Law No. 5510, employment and social security laws and any amendments to them, and legal precedents. Our services covers drafting and termination of employment agreements in scope of the Maritime Labour Act and the Press Labor Law.

Today, the Social Security Institution renews the social security legislation according to the needs to provide recruitment and control the on-going processes. We know that social security is an area that is open to development and where the changes happen so fast and our specialists support our clients with this understanding.

We provide service to our clients in all transactions that are being done in the Social Security Institution. We have experience in various fields such as recruiting and removing employees, monitoring the premiums to be paid to the Social Security Institution.

Data Protection and Data Governance

Our experienced and specialized team aims to provide first class service to our clients, within the framework of the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698, EU General Data Protection Regulation and the relevant legislation regarding the area.

With the increasing use of technology, the misuse of data has been improved enourmously which created the need of a regulatory action to be taken. Even the most equipped companies can take the false steps and cannot comply with the constantly evolving legislation.

Our team keep abreast of the legislative changes not only in Turkey but also in Europe (e.g. Data Governance Act, Digital Services Act, Data Act) and keep our clients up to date so that they are prepared for the changes that may be made in Turkey in the future in accordance with the European legislation.

E-Commerce

As a result of the latest experiences in the fields of health, technology and economy in recent years, online commerce has revived which has led to an increase in disputes regarding consumers using the e-commerce environment.

Product and service providers on the internet must take certain measures and comply with the legislation regarding e-commerce, such as the Law on the Regulation of Electronic Commerce No. 6563, Regulation on Service Providers and Intermediate Service Providers in Electronic Commerce and Regulation on Commercial Communication and Commercial Electronic Messages.

With this awareness, our team ensures that our clients’ websites and mobile applications are complied with the legislation, providing a safe online environment. With a serious rate of success, we provide high-quality service to our clients in disputes with their consumers and follow the processes meticulously.

Media and Advertisement

Our highly experienced team provides services in areas such as domain names, social media, advertising, marketing and electronic marketing.

In order to provide the best service to our clients, we closely monitor all legislative changes made in Europe and Turkey and ensure compliance with these changes. By having a detailed and meticulous work ethic, we offer our clients the most accurate solutions in a short amount of time.
